How To Use Our Lemon Fresh
Dishwash Liquid

Using our Lemon Fresh Dishwash Liquid is easy and straightforward. Follow these simple steps to achieve a sparkling clean kitchen with minimal effort.

Prepare Your Dishes

Gather all your dirty dishes and scrape off any leftover food into the trash. Rinse the dishes lightly to remove loose debris.

Apply the Dishwash Liquid

Add a few drops of our Dishwash Liquid onto a sponge or directly onto the dish. You can also mix a small amount of the liquid with warm water in a sink or basin.

Wash and Rinse

Scrub the dishes thoroughly with a sponge or cloth, covering all surfaces. Rinse under running water until all soap is gone.
